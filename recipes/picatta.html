Veal Piccata

Description

Veal Piccata has long been attested by our local patrons as the number 4 dish they would recommend to a prisoner on death row. Have a go at demolishing this recipe and discovering that you too can cook. So go ahead and find the nearest farm with a calf and get to cooking...

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless veal round
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 1/2 cups beef broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 8 thin slices lemon

Steps

  1. Rub the veal pieces with the salt, pepper, flour, and oregano. Heat the oil over moderate-low heat in a 12-inch skillet.
  2. Add the garlic and onion and saute for 3-5 minutes or until soft but not browned. With a slotted spoon remove the onion and garlic and set aside.
  3. Return the onion and garlic to the pan and add the broth. Bring to a boil, cover, then turn to simmer for 10-15 minutes. Stir in the lemon juice and coat the veal slices evenly with the juice.
  4. Arrange on a heated serving platter and garnish with the lemon slices, capers, and parsley.
