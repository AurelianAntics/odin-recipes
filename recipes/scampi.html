Shrimp Scampi

Description

Pack with all sorts of flavor from the sea, this shrimp scampi will be discussed at all future social gatherings that happen to involve people of rather high social status. Add some of your own spices to give it a distinct regional flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbs olive oil
  • 4 Tbs butter
  • 4-5 large minced garlic
  • 1 1/4 lbs large shrimp prawn, shelled with tailes on or off
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine or broth
  • That secret spice

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil and butter in large pan or skillet
  2. pour in wine. Bring to simmer for 1-2 minutes or until wine reduces by about half and the shrimp is cooked through.
  3. Stir in remaining butter, lemon juice, parsley and take off heat immediately.
  4. Stir over pasta.
