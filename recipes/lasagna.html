Lasagna

Description

This dish will easily serve the whole family for 3 days and nights. No one can resist the power of a delicious dish of lasagna strewn out uponi the dining table. Pour a glass of wine and break some bread and try out this unique lasanage recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground meat
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta and mozarella cheeese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven 375 degrees F
  2. Boil large pot of salt water
  3. Cook pasta
  4. Assemble meat sauce
  5. Spread meat sauce and cheese
  6. Heat in prebaked oven for 25 minutes
